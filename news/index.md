# Changelog

## pgatouR 0.1.0

Initial release.

### Functions

#### Live Tournament Data

- [`pga_leaderboard()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_leaderboard.md)
  — Full leaderboard with scores, positions, and round-by-round results
- [`pga_current_leaders()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_current_leaders.md)
  — Quick top-15 snapshot for in-progress tournaments
- [`pga_tee_times()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_tee_times.md)
  — Tee time groupings with start tees and player assignments
- [`pga_scorecard()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_scorecard.md)
  — Hole-by-hole scorecard with par, score, yardage, and status
- [`pga_shot_details()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_shot_details.md)
  — Shot-by-shot tracking data with coordinates and play-by-play
- [`pga_odds()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_odds.md)
  — Betting odds to win for the tournament field
- [`pga_coverage()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_coverage.md)
  — Broadcast and streaming schedule

#### Statistics & Standings

- [`pga_stats()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_stats.md)
  — Any of 300+ stats with full player rankings (2004-2026)
- [`pga_fedex_cup()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_fedex_cup.md)
  — FedExCup standings with projected and official rankings
- [`pga_scorecard_comparison()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_scorecard_comparison.md)
  — Head-to-head stat comparison between players

#### Players & Tournaments

- [`pga_players()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_players.md)
  — Full player directory (2,400+ players across PGA Tour, Champions,
  Korn Ferry)
- [`pga_tournaments()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_tournaments.md)
  — Tournament metadata including location, courses, weather, and format
- [`pga_schedule()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_schedule.md)
  — Full season schedule with dates, purse, course, champion, and
  FedExCup points

#### Content

- [`pga_news()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_news.md)
  — News articles with filtering and pagination
- [`pga_news_franchises()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_news_franchises.md)
  — Available news categories
- [`pga_videos()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_videos.md)
  — Player video highlights
- [`pga_tourcast_videos()`](https://walrusquant.github.io/pgatouR/reference/pga_tourcast_videos.md)
  — Shot-by-shot video clips

#### Data

- `stat_ids` — Bundled lookup table of 340 stat IDs with names,
  categories, and subcategories
